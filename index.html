Marvel Quiz Rules

  • Once You Enter the quiz, you will be asked to select a difficulty. These difficulties range from easy all the way up to hard. The harder the difficulty, the more specific the questions.
  • After you have selected a difficulty, you will be presented with 5 questions. You must answer all 5 questions to continue.
  • After answering all questions you will recieve a score, you can replay the quiz to get a perfect score or move on to the harder difficulties.
